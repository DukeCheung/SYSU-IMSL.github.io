    • The Intelligent and Multimedia Science Laboratory in Sun Yat-sen University specializes in the researches and applications of the cross-domain hybrid of intelligent science and multimedia.
    In particular, the research topics include: computer vision (vision-based multimodal indoor localization, animated objects analysis and detection, image adversarial attack) and computer graphics (graphics and image processing, garment modeling and virtual try-on, 3d rendering and modeling), etc.
